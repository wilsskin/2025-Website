Bodega Bay Lavender E-Commerce Platform

2025 · Shipped

CONTEXT

Built a new e-commerce platform for Bodega Bay Lavender

As a freelancer, I owned the site redesign from end-to-end and implemented it with HTML/CSS and Javascript, increasing 2025 holiday sales by 400%.

Bodega Bay Lavender is a family-owned ranch in California that grows and distills lavender to create a variety of wellness products. The startup primarily relied on in-person revenue channels and lacked a strong online store so I was commissioned to create a more effective Shopify e-commerce platform.

PROBLEM

The old site wasn’t attracting or converting shoppers

With fewer than 3500 annual users and a conversion rate below 1%, the old platform needed fixing. Through a UX audit and a few user interviews I discovered the main issues were weak trust signals, no clear value proposition, and a lack of storytelling. A cluttered site map and weak SEO also limited sales potential.

GOAL

Create a more engaging shopping experience and increase online sales

I focused on improving shopper confidence by adding social proof, crafting a compelling value proposition and weaving in storytelling about the brand.

How might we gain shopper trust and encourage them to purchase lavender products from Bodega Bay Lavender?

SOLUTION

I designed and implemented a new Shopify platform to improve trust and encourage shopping

The new site provides shoppers with a clearer understanding of the brand, highlights key lavender benefits, and creates a more natural path from browsing to purchasing.
