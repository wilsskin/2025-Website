Designed and Developed a New E-Commerce Platform from 0→1 to Increase Product Sales

Bodega Bay Lavender is a family-owned ranch in Northern California that grows and distills lavender to create a variety of wellness products.

As the only contributor, I created and implemented a new Shopify e-commerce platform using HTML/CSS to increase online sales and address a 22% drop in conversions. The updated site was launched on November 7th and I’m currently conducting a final round of user testing to gather feedback.

PROBLEM

The old site wasn’t attracting or converting shoppers

Low traffic, weak trust signals, and a lack of storytelling made it hard for shoppers to understand the brand or feel confident making a purchase.

Less than 3500 annual visitors

Weak SEO and a lack of storytelling made it difficult to attract new users

Less than 1% of users made a purchase

A cluttered site map without trust signals left shoppers unsure about purchasing

No clear value proposition

Users couldn’t tell why they should buy or what made the brand unique

GOAL

Create a more inviting shopping experience

How might we help shoppers better understand the brand and convince them to purchase lavender products?

1

Attract more users to the platform

2

Increase shopper confidence and conversions

3

Communicate a clear, compelling value proposition

SOLUTION

I designed and built a new site to effectively guide shoppers

The new platform gives shoppers a clearer understanding of the brand, highlights key products, and creates a more natural path from browsing to purchasing.
